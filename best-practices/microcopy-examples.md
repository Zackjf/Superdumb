# Microcopy Examples

Before/after pairs organized by context. Use these as a starting reference, then adapt to your product's voice.

## Button Labels

| Before (vague) | After (specific) | Why |
|----------------|-------------------|-----|
| Submit | Create account | Says what actually happens. |
| Click here | Download invoice | Describes the outcome, not the action. |
| OK | Save changes | "OK" tells users nothing about what will happen. |
| Yes | Delete project | In confirmations, repeat the action on the button. |

## Form Field Labels

| Before | After | Why |
|--------|-------|-----|
| Name | Full name | Removes ambiguity (first? last? company?). |
| Address | Street address | Distinguishes from email address or billing address. |
| Phone | Mobile number (for 2FA codes) | Explains why you need it. |
| Message | How can we help? | Conversational and guides the user on what to write. |

## Validation Errors

| Before | After | Why |
|--------|-------|-----|
| Invalid input | Email must include @ and a domain (e.g., you@company.com) | Shows the rule and a correct example. |
| Error | Password needs at least 8 characters -- you have 5 | Tells them how far off they are. |
| Required field | Company name is required to create your workspace | Explains why it matters. |
| Invalid date | Start date must be today or later | States the rule in plain language. |

## Empty States

| Before | After | Why |
|--------|-------|-----|
| No data | No projects yet. Create your first project to get started. | Tells them what to do next. |
| 0 results | You haven't sent any invoices yet. [Send your first invoice] | Provides a direct action. |
| Nothing here | No team members added. Invite your team to start collaborating. | Frames the value of taking action. |
| Empty | Your notifications will appear here when someone mentions you or comments on your work. | Explains when this will have content. |

## Loading States

| Before | After | Why |
|--------|-------|-----|
| Loading... | Loading your dashboard... | Tells them what is loading. |
| Please wait | Generating your report -- this usually takes 10-15 seconds | Sets time expectations. |
| (spinner only) | Saving your changes... | Any spinner without text creates anxiety. |
| Processing | Connecting to your bank -- do not close this window | Tells them what to expect and what NOT to do. |

## Success Messages

| Before | After | Why |
|--------|-------|-----|
| Success | Payment received -- you'll get a receipt at alex@mail.com | Confirms the outcome with specifics. |
| Done | Profile updated. Your new name will appear across the app within a minute. | Sets expectations about what happens next. |
| Saved | Draft saved at 2:34 PM. [Preview] [Continue editing] | Timestamp + next actions. |
| Submitted | Application submitted. We'll review it within 2 business days and email you. | Gives a timeline. |

## Destructive Action Confirmations

| Before | After | Why |
|--------|-------|-----|
| Are you sure? | Delete "Q3 Marketing Plan"? This removes all 12 tasks and cannot be undone. | Names the item and states consequences. |
| Confirm | Remove Sarah from the team? She will lose access to all projects immediately. | Specific about who and what happens. |
| Yes / No | [Cancel] / [Delete 3 files permanently] | Button labels describe outcomes, not just yes/no. |
| Warning | Downgrading to Free removes API access and custom domains. Your data stays. [Keep Pro] / [Downgrade to Free] | States what they lose AND what they keep. |

## Tooltips / Helper Text

| Before | After | Why |
|--------|-------|-----|
| Enter your API key | Find your API key in Settings > Integrations > API Keys | Tells them WHERE to find it. |
| Choose a plan | You can change your plan anytime from Settings | Reduces commitment anxiety. |
| Select a date | Billing happens on this date each month | Explains the consequence of the choice. |
| Upload a file | PNG, JPG, or SVG. Max 2 MB. Recommended: 400x400px | All constraints in one place. |

## Navigation Labels

| Before | After | Why |
|--------|-------|-----|
| Miscellaneous | More tools | "Miscellaneous" signals lazy IA. |
| Admin | Team settings | Describes what's inside, not who it's for. |
| Hub | Projects | Generic "hub" means nothing. Use the noun. |
| Content | Blog posts | Specific is always better than abstract. |

## Search States

| Before | After | Why |
|--------|-------|-----|
| No results | No results for "acme." Try a different spelling or search for a broader term. | Acknowledges the query and gives next steps. |
| 0 matches found | No customers match "acme." [Search all contacts instead?] | Offers to widen the search. |
| Not found | We couldn't find "acme" in invoices. It might be in [Estimates] or [Contacts]. | Suggests other places to look. |
| Try again | No results. Popular searches: pricing, integrations, API docs | Gives starting points when users are stuck. |

## Permission Requests

| Before | After | Why |
|--------|-------|-----|
| Allow notifications? | Get notified when your team comments on your work? | Frames as a benefit, not a system request. |
| Allow camera access | Take a profile photo? We need camera access for this. | Explains why right before they need it. |
| Allow location | Show stores near you? We'll use your location just for this search. | Limits scope to reduce concern. |
| Enable cookies | We use cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. | Explains the actual benefit to the user. |

## 404 / Error Pages

| Before | After | Why |
|--------|-------|-----|
| 404 Not Found | This page doesn't exist. It may have been moved or deleted. | Plain language, possible explanation. |
| Internal Server Error | Something went wrong on our end. We've been notified. Try again in a few minutes. | Takes responsibility and sets expectations. |
| Forbidden | You don't have access to this page. [Request access] or [Go to dashboard] | Gives actionable options. |
| Bad Gateway | We're having trouble connecting right now. Your data is safe. Try refreshing. | Addresses anxiety (is my data okay?) and suggests action. |
