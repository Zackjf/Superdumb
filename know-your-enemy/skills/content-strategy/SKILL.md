# Content Strategy Analysis

Don't assume every site needs a blog. Don't assume every site needs case studies. Look at what actually exists in the market and what ranks.

## What to Analyze

### Blog analysis
- How many competitors have a blog? (count)
- How many are active? (posted in last 90 days)
- What topics do they cover?
- Do blog posts rank for any keywords? (check Google: `site:competitor.com/blog`)
- If fewer than 3 of 15 actively blog, and no blog content ranks → recommend skipping

### FAQ analysis
- How many competitors have FAQ pages?
- What questions do they answer?
- Search Google for "[business type] [city]" — does "People Also Ask" appear?
- Compile the top 10-15 questions people ask about this business type
- If FAQ sites capture more traffic → recommend building one

### Video/media
- Do competitors embed video? On which pages?
- What kind? (testimonials, facility tours, explainers, procedure demos)
- Is YouTube a discovery channel for this niche?

### Other content
- Case studies / success stories
- Guides / resources / downloads
- Before/after galleries
- Newsletters
- Calculators or interactive tools

## Output

A content recommendation with evidence:

```
CONTENT STRATEGY:

Blog: NO
  3 of 15 competitors blog. 2 abandoned (last post 8+ months ago).
  Blog content does not rank for local dental keywords.
  Recommendation: skip. Invest in service pages and FAQ instead.

FAQ: YES
  6 of 15 have FAQ pages. Those 6 rank for 3x more long-tail keywords.
  Top questions from People Also Ask + competitor FAQs:
  1. "¿Cuánto cuesta una limpieza dental?"
  2. "¿Duele un tratamiento de conducto?"
  3. "¿Cada cuánto debo ir al dentista?"
  ...

Before/After Gallery: RECOMMENDED
  5 of 15 have galleries. All 5 are in the top 8 rankings.
  High engagement signal for dental specifically.

Video: OPTIONAL
  3 of 15 embed video. No ranking correlation.
  If the dentist wants to do video, embed on service pages.
  Don't build a video section as a priority.
```
